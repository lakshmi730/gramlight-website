Women Empowerment Initiative

At Gramlight, we believe that true progress comes from empowering communities. Our women empowerment program is at the heart of our mission. We provide comprehensive training to women in rural areas, teaching them LED manufacturing skills, business management, and entrepreneurship. Through our program, these women gain financial independence and become job creators in their communities.

Our training modules cover technical skills, quality control, marketing, and financial management, ensuring that every graduate is well-equipped to start and manage their own LED manufacturing business successfully.
